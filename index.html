How does it work?

Read all information or you can try Marvel demo of DrinkLink client app

Check out democlient app

Choose the place

Search by name, location or select a place from the list. Take note of timings and days DrinkLink is active as well as the collection time.

Select drinks

Browse the menu, choose a drink and quantity. Table service or pickup. You can even send your friends a round from home, just forward them the Code.

Pay order

Enter your card details or just CVS number if the card was saved before. After order is accepted payment is processed through reputable payment gateway.

Follow progress

Monitor stages and receive customised alerts, rings or vibrations, for each change. When the order is ready, countdown timer will appear.

Collect

Show the Code to your server at the designated DrinkLink collection point, free of crowd and commotion. If delivered, show the Code at your table.

About Us

DrinkLink is a mobile application that lets you request, pay and receive your order at the premises of different beverage serving establishments or events. Together with array of other digital products and popular apps, DrinkLink is run and owned by PopApp FZ LLC, founded in 2018 and based in Dubai. PopApp’s business model is built around people's common needs and we aim to fulfil those requirements through a range of digital solutions.
